ABOUT US

A very warm welcome from Vijay packers and movers. We are known for providing the best relocation services from Pune to all over India. We are a moving company having specialization in providing the services which are house shifting, office relocation, vehicle shifting, corporate relocation, packing & unpacking, loading & unloading etc.
We have gained huge recognition as a trustworthy company to work with because of the transparency that we build with our customers from the first meeting itself.
At Vijay packers and movers, we use high quality material to pack and maintain the safety of your items. We are proud to say that our team of skilled professionals and their deep and extensive knowledge helps us to provide the best service to our clients. 

As our testimonials reflects, we are bound to complete the work on time and without any hassle. Vijay packers and movers is one among the leading packers and movers service providers in Pune having years of experience. We assure our clients that they will receive their items in the same condition as they expected with no damage with our three level packaging method. Safety of your items is the top most priority at Vijay packers and movers. We provide 24x7 efficient client support. The services are rendered in compliance with international standards and our zero-tolerance policy regarding packing and moving of consignments and timely delivery also helps us in attaining complete satisfaction of our clients.

Considering the client's requirements, we enhance the number of vehicles in our feet. In order to assure client's about the safety of their goods, we also make avail to them the status of vehicles on service so that they can keep tract of their items. In case of any accident or unexpected damage occurs during the transportation, then we take the full responsibility and try to compensate the losses caused to clients.
